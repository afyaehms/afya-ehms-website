The Afya System


A comprehensive Electronic Health Management System built on open source technologies

Our Vision

The Health Sector is embracing the use of IT solutions to improve efficiency and quality of service delivery. As a result, a number of different IT systems and solutions are in use in the health sector, from community, facility, and management levels. These are each used to manage services and systems to improve delivery and quality of care.

The solution consists of a comprehensive Electronic Health Management System built on open source technologies as well as tools for the transmission of healthcare-related data among facilities, health information organizations and government agencies according to national standards.

Implementation Status

Afya EHMS is currently being implemented in six counties around Kenya.

Our Partners

To address the need to use of IT solutions to improve efficiency and quality of service delivery, a consultancy was commissioned by WHO and the Kenya Ministry of Health to develop an Electronic Health Records system (EHRS) that will allow the interoperability of the various systems and support the movement of the health information as and when needed.
